If you see something, say something

I was abandoned on a seat on the IRT #1 train inside of a black backpack. The conductor was informed of a suspicious package and the train halted. They cautiously lifted open the unzipped backpack and found me amid a slew of restaurant kitchen towels which had the fresh imprints of all of my siblings. I was then rescued by a passenger in the crowd who snuck me into her office inside of an UGG sheepskin hat and eventually, I went home with that girl.
